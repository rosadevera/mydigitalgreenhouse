SONNY!

✿ Common name: Sunflower

✿ Botanical name: Helianthus

✿ Plant type: Asteraceae

✿ Sun exposure required: Full

✿ Water care: Well-draining

✿ Native area: North America (West Coast of the US and Mexico)

11.03.2023

October has quickly passed already and its now fall/winter season truly!


To celebrate the start of November and the holiday season soon approaching, my sister and I decided to buy some new flowers for to decorate our apartment with! At Trader Joe's there was a lovely selection of different flowers to pick from-- we decided to choose something red, orange, or yellow as they are fall colors, so we got these lovely sunflowers!


Although flowers don't last very long, Sonny is a great pop of color in our living room and gives me some home for the next few weeks :)
