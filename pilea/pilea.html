PILEA!

✿ Common name: Chinese Money Plant

✿ Botanical name: Pilea Peperomioides

✿ Plant type: Perennial

✿ Sun exposure required: Partial

✿ Water care: Well-draining

✿ Native area: China

08.26.2023

On August 28th, the day we finally moved into our new apartment, my sister gifted me the offspring of her own money plant as a gift! I will make sure to take good care of her...

10.04.2023

It's been a while! Pilea is thriving and growing well, lots of babies/offspring are growing in her base... its lovely to see! I'm still a plant newbie so I am not really sure at what point you should transfer the plants to a bigger and newer pot but I think I will keep her here for now.

11.21.2023

My poor child... something fell of my windowsill and broke Pilea T_T


I don't have the heart to dispose of the part of the plant that broke off... so I just kept the piece inside the pot... maybe she'll start growing again who knows... so sad...
