POTHOS!

✿ Common name: Devil's Ivy

✿ Botanical name: Golden Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

✿ Plant type: Araceae, Vine

✿ Sun exposure required: Full sun, partial shade

✿ Water care: Moist but well-drained

✿ Native area: Asia, Oceania

10.10.2023

Not a recent addition to the family but a new addition to my digital greenhouse!


This Pothos has been with me since my last apartment, and (thankfully) has survived the move. She is growing so beautifully and is always a nice addition to the shelves at our place due to its long vines/leaves that grow downwards and drape beautifully over furniture.


Though looking at her now, I've noticed that her leaves have many different colors, probably due to the different amount of sunlight she gets. Maybe once in a while I should rotate her position so that the golden color of her leaves shines more in other parts as well!
