ROSA!

✿ Botanical name: Rosa

✿ Plant type: Rosaceae

✿ Sun exposure required: Medium

09.23.2023

Happy Birthday to me! Part of the annual tradition, I have received some beautiful flowers once again from my lovely parents and sister


My birthday this year clearly had a pink theme going on-- I got two pretty pink rose bouquets! One purely just pink roses in a jar from my sister, and the other in a lovely floral arrangement with lilies, chrysanthemums, baby’s breath, and more. All very rosa-core truly!


Unfortunately however, flowers are not the same as plants and don’t last as long-- I can seem them slowly dying as the days go by. At least with this digital greenhouse my beautiful roses can live on forever in this virtual space!
